Rymac Technical Solutions Inc.


Rymac Technical Solutions Inc., or Rymac, is an Alberta, Canada based technology company that helps reduce the time and resources small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs) and individuals spend managing large collections of collectible items.

Rymac’s approach uses a computer vision model trained using Artificial Intelligence (AI) to replace the extensive manual labour required by these businesses to process items, operations that involve sorting, cataloging, storing, and ultimately retrieval.

The primary focus of Rymac’s business is to reduce the inefficiencies in the emerging niche market of collectible items which is currently burdened by outdated methodologies. This market is occupied by a rapidly expanding, yet close knit, community which is filled with extremely passionate consumers and SMEs. It is Rymac’s desire to assist these businesses and individuals grow alongside their market.
